Welcome to our Vegan Kithen

Vegan Kitchen is an optional webpage from Minimalist Baker. This webpage is a part of final project during programming class from CodeFirst:Girl. The content of this website has been modified based on Minimalist Baker webstite. The Minimalist Baker is a website devoted to simple cooking. All recipes require 10 ingredients or less, 1 bowl or 1 pot, or 30 minutes or less to prepare. This Minimalist Baker has been founded by Dana and John in 2012. The Minimalist Baker is currently situated in Portland, Oregon.

The Recipes

Because everyone can benefit from incorporating more plants into their diets, our recipes are special-diet friendly, specifically plant-based and gluten-free.
